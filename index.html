About Us

At AlgoGenex, we're dedicated to transforming lead-to-revenue conversion for companies with intelligent, hands-free automation. Too many companies leave money on the table by not reaching out to new and existing leads. We leverage sophisticated AI to be your 24/7 "sales rep," reaching out to leads via SMS, chat widgets, and social DMs and then booking qualified appointments right onto your schedule.

We believe in an outcome-based approach: you pay only when our AI actually delivers tangible results. No retainer fees. No hourly billing. Every campaign is customized to your unique brand tone, CRM setup, and sales process, so the bot can talk and act like one of your employees. Regardless of whether you're a local merchant, an e-commerce store, or a B2B consultant, we partner with you to design, develop, and optimize your AI selling system to consistently maximize performance.

Our purpose is to treat your success as our own. With transparent reporting and regular performance reviews, we will make sure your AI never stops getting better. Welcome to the future of selling, welcome to AlgoGenex.
